About me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the FAIR NY) lab, where I am currently working on unsupervised text summarization, long form question answering, and natural language interactions between human and machine.

After an undergrad at Ecole Polytechnique, and earning a Master in Machine Learning from ENS Cachan, I completed my PhD at New York University in January 2018 under the supervision of Prof. David Sontag, during which I worked on language modeling, graphical models, and medical applications of natural language processing.
